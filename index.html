About Me

Hi, I'm Flavia Kosti, a Frontend Developer with over 7 years of experience crafting engaging, high-performance, and responsive user interfaces. My passion lies in transforming great designs into seamless digital experiences, ensuring that every interaction is intuitive, efficient, and visually compelling.

I bring strong problem-solving skills and a keen eye for design, ensuring that every interface is both functional and visually appealing. I excel at translating business needs into highly functional, user-friendly applications that enhance user experience and drive engagement. Passionate about staying ahead of industry trends, I continuously explore new technologies to build cutting-edge web applications.

With a proven track record in fast-paced, agile environments, I adapt quickly to evolving project requirements and consistently deliver high-quality solutions. My collaborative approach allows me to work effectively with designers, developers, and stakeholders, bringing ideas to life through seamless teamwork and clear communication.

Throughout my career, I’ve had the privilege of working with diverse teams across various industries, contributing to projects that range from fast-growing startups to enterprise-level applications. I take pride in writing clean, efficient, and maintainable code, ensuring that my work not only meets but exceeds client expectations.

If you're looking for a dedicated frontend developer to bring your vision to life—or simply want to connect and exchange ideas—I’d love to hear from you!
